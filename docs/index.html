WordStat Ontology

WordStat is a linguistic ontology that defines a schema for structuring linguistic data, supporting NLP, AI-driven text analysis, and vocabulary enrichment.

@prefix ws: <https://purl.archive.org/wordstat/> .

Version

Current version: 0.0.9 (Draft). Future refinements will follow semantic versioning.

Classes and Properties

Context

A linguistic unit representing a broader section of text, such as a paragraph or chapter.

:text1 a ws:Context .

MainContext

Defines the primary context in a dataset. There can be only one per set.

:document1 a ws:MainContext .

Has SubContext

Defines a hierarchical relationship where a larger linguistic unit contains a smaller one.

:chapter1 ws:hasSubContext :paragraph2 .

Sub Context Of

Inverse relation of hasSubContext, defining a bottom-up linguistic structure.

:paragraph2 ws:subContextOf :chapter1 .

Follows Zipf

Indicates whether a text adheres to Zipf’s law regarding word frequency.

:corpus1 ws:followsZipf true .

Type-Token Ratio (TTR)

Measures lexical diversity in a text.

:document1 ws:ttr "0.58"^^xsd:decimal .

Token

The smallest meaningful linguistic unit.

:word1 a ws:Token ; rdfs:label "analysis" .

Function Words

Words with primarily grammatical functions.

:word1 a ws:functionWords .

Content Words

Words carrying significant meaning.

:word2 a ws:contentWords .

In Context

Indicates the context in which a token appears.

:word1 ws:inContext :chapter1 .

Sibling Context With

Indicates a shared characteristic between two contexts.

:paragraph1 ws:siblingContextWith :paragraph2 .

Count In Context

Represents the number of occurrences of a token within a specific context.

:word1 ws:countInContext "15"^^xsd:integer .

Percentage In Context

Represents a token’s frequency relative to the total number of tokens.

:word1 ws:percentageInContext "0.02"^^xsd:decimal .

Is Function Word

Classifies a word as a function word.

:word1 ws:isFunctionWord true .

Is Content Word

Classifies a word as a content word.

:word2 ws:isContentWord true .

Noun

A lexical category representing objects, people, or concepts.

:word3 ws:POS ws:Noun .

Verb

A lexical category representing actions or states.

:word4 ws:POS ws:Verb .

Pronoun

A word that replaces a noun in discourse.

:word5 ws:POS ws:Pronoun .

Adjective

Describes or modifies a noun.

:word6 ws:POS ws:Adjective .

Adverb

Modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.

:word7 ws:POS ws:Adverb .

Preposition

Defines spatial or temporal relationships.

:word8 ws:POS ws:Preposition .

Conjunction

Connects linguistic units.

:word9 ws:POS ws:Conjunction .

Interjection

Expresses emotions or reactions.

:word10 ws:POS ws:Interjection .

Determiner

Specifies a noun’s reference.

:word11 ws:POS ws:Determiner .

Conclusion

WordStat provides a structured ontology for AI-driven text analysis, vocabulary enrichment, and NLP applications.
